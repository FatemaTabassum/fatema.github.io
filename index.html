Understanding Misleading Charts and Data Literacy

Today I explored how misleading charts can influence decision-making and how to read data more critically. Understanding data visualization is essential for making informed decisions in research and industry.

Key takeaways:

  • Always question the data source
  • Check axes and scaling before trusting trends
  • Avoid misleading chart design
  • Use clear visualization to communicate truthfully
